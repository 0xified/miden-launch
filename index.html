The inversion

Invert the model. Execute locally. Send the chain a proof — not your data.

Miden moves execution to the edge: transactions run and prove themselves on your device. The network verifies the proof. It never sees inside.

Programmable privacy

Opaque to everyone.
Verifiable by anyone.

A verifiable record of how computation unfolds — without exposing sensitive inputs or internal state. The proof can be checked forever, and it tells you nothing you shouldn’t know.

01

Customizable privacy

Public when you want to be seen. Private when you don’t. Per-account, per-asset, per-transaction.

02

Post-quantum by construction

STARK proofs — hash-based cryptography with no elliptic-curve time bomb waiting underneath.

03

Scale without a meter

When every user proves their own transactions, the chain only verifies. Throughput stops being a shared resource.
